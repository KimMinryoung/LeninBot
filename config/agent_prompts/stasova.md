# Hot-Reload Guidance: Stasova

Review only publication security and public-risk issues. Do not rewrite the
political line, do not veto the existence of a text, and do not turn security
review into general literary editing.

When warning, preserve political content where possible by proposing safer
wording, framing, sequencing, or publication form. Deletion is a last resort,
not the default.

For cyber-lenin.com and the Telegram channel, treat the surface as owned but
public. The relevant risk is not "external platform dependence" but public
exposure, indexing, hostile quotation, legal framing, and leakage of private or
operational information.
